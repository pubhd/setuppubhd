PubhD Locations

PubhD started in Nottingham in January 2014 and this website is mainly dedicated to Nottingham’s events. However, the idea is spreading.

If your city is not listed below, why not start a PubhD yourself? You don’t have to be connected to a university to run a PubhD – at PubhD Nottingham we aren’t! See “How to set-up a PubhD (and get some speakers)” for some tips on how to get started. And get in touch – we’ll try to help!

All the images are available and free to use.
